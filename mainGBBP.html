About the Government Business Blockchain Platform

The Government Business Blockchain Platform (GBBP) is a multi-blockchain platform that provides a common environment for GBA members, chapters and working groups to develop and deploy blockchain applications for government. The GBBP supports gateways to various blockchains and applications that can transport information and assets across the connected blockchains, forming a Government Blockchain Marketplace.
